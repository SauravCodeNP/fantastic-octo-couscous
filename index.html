About Us

Bhanudaya Model Secondary School, located in Dumkibas, Nawalpur, is a leading institution dedicated to academic excellence and innovation. The school provides quality education in Science, Management, and Humanities, preparing students for a bright future. With experienced faculty, modern facilities, and a focus on practical learning, Bhanudaya Model Secondary School nurtures young minds to become future leaders, engineers, scientists, and professionals. The school promotes discipline, creativity, and leadership, encouraging students to engage in extracurricular activities, research, and technology-driven learning. Through a student-centered approach, it ensures a well-rounded education that meets global standards.
